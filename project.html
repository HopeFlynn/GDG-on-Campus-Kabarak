GDG (Google Developer Groups) on Campus is a community of students and tech enthusiasts at Kabarak University who are passionate about learning and sharing knowledge in technology, web development, machine learning, mobile development, and other Google technologies.

You can join by attending our meetups, events, or workshops. Membership is open to all Kabarak University students. Simply join our WhatsApp Group and check out our Events page to see the next opportunity to get involved.

We organize a variety of activities, including hands-on coding workshops, hackathons, tech talks, and collaborative projects. We also host annual events like Google I/O Extended and DevFest to help students engage with the latest trends in technology.

No, our events are designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced developers. Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your knowledge, we have something for everyone.
