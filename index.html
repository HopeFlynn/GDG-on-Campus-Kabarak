Our Fields

We focus on multiple areas of technology and beyond, including:

Web Development

We specialize in crafting dynamic, responsive websites and web applications that deliver exceptional user experiences. Whether you're looking to build a sleek, interactive front-end or a robust back-end, we help you bring your vision to life.

Android Development

Our Android development team creates intuitive and powerful mobile applications for the Android platform. From developing user-friendly interfaces to implementing complex functionality.

Data Science and Machine Learning

We dive deep into data to uncover actionable insights and create predictive models. Our data scientists and ML specialists harness the power of data to drive intelligent decisions and develop advanced algorithms for a range of applications.

IoT and Robotics

In the realm of IoT and Robotics, we innovate by connecting smart devices and designing intelligent robots. We integrate sensors and control systems to develop solutions that enhance automation and connectivity, bridging the physical and digital worlds.

Cybersecurity

We are dedicated to safeguarding your digital assets. Our cybersecurity experts protect against threats and vulnerabilities, ensuring your systems and data remain secure. We focus on proactive measures and innovative solutions to keep your information safe.

Nontechnical Fields

We also focus on essential nontechnical skills that complement technical expertise. These include:

  • Technical Writing
  • Project Management
  • Communication Skills
  • Leadership

Our Projects

Explore Our Latest Projects

EVIS

EVIS is an electronic Voting Information System.The vision behind EVIS is to eliminate the inefficiencies and challenges associated with traditional voting methods, such as long queues, manual errors, and security concerns
Mama's hub

Mama's hub is an application for pregnant mothers that helps them track their health progress throughout their pregnancy journey through an app and a smart watch.

Amema Crop

Amema Crop application is set to transform farming not only in Kenya but across Africa and the globe by offering smart solutions for crop diseases, a blockchain-based microfinance platform, and expert learning content.

GDG (Google Developer Groups) on Campus is a community of students and tech enthusiasts at Kabarak University who are passionate about learning and sharing knowledge in technology, web development, machine learning, mobile development, and other Google technologies.

You can join by attending our meetups, events, or workshops. Membership is open to all Kabarak University students. Simply join our WhatsApp Group and check out our Events page to see the next opportunity to get involved.

We organize a variety of activities, including hands-on coding workshops, hackathons, tech talks, and collaborative projects. We also host annual events like Google I/O Extended and DevFest to help students engage with the latest trends in technology.
